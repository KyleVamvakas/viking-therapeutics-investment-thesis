## Viking Therapeutics Investment Thesis

**Date:** July 1, 2025

This project presents an investment thesis on Viking Therapeutics (VKTX), a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing drugs for metabolic diseases.

The analysis focuses on Viking’s two lead assets:
- **VK2735** (obesity, GLP-1/GIP dual agonist)
- **VK2809** (NASH, TRβ agonist)

A probability-adjusted net present value (pNPV) framework is used to estimate valuation, incorporating assumptions on peak sales, probability of success, timelines, and profitability. Sensitivity analysis is performed on key inputs such as discount rate and P/E multiple.

The model yields:
- **Base case:** $35.09/share  
- **Valuation range:** $23.35 – $61.11/share  

Overall, the thesis highlights strong clinical data, large market opportunities, and asymmetric upside driven by VK2735, supporting a **Buy** recommendation.
